Blender

The Software Behind Greg's Creation

Blender is the 3D modeling software we studied in our MMV course. We started by
familiarizing ourselves with Blender's interface and basic tools. Gradually, we
progressed to more advanced techniques, learning how to model, texture, and
animate a character from scratch. Here is a roadmap of our journey, highlighting
the key steps we took to create and bring Greg to life using Blender.

Basic Modeling in Blender

We explored the basics of the modeling tool Blender. We got to know
the interface, the organization of tools, and the workflow in the tool. We undertook
the creation of a basic 3-dimensional model using fundamental modeling approaches.

Modeling and Rigging

We learned how to model using a reference image. We worked on creating
a low-poly model and refined it as much as possible to prepare it for animation.
Next, we revised and prepared the model from what we previously did, ensuring it was
suitable for animation by adding a skeleton or armature and completing the rigging process.

Animation

We learned the basics of animation using our existing model. We started by understanding keyframe animation and how to set up a timeline in Blender. We practiced creating simple animations, such as walking and idle, to bring our low-poly model to life. Next, we refined our animations to make them smoother and more realistic.
